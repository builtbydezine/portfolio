Hello I'm Parth Gupta.


Currently a Product Designer at PaySense, experienced with UX, UI, Front-end & Visual Design.

I am a Product Designer, working and living in Mumbai, India. Having learned the principles of design and human behaviour in my Bachelor’s degree of Architecture, I decided to work in the creative field of UX/UI Design to follow my love of technology along with the touch of art and psychology. I really focus upon UX but practice branding, visual design and front-end development for personal projects.

Last year, I started working as a full-time Product Designer at Paysense to learn the agile process of bootstrapping a product from scratch, gain insights and refine every detail based on it. Involved in the entire design process, from visualizing product features, determining the target audience, visualizing UX flows, creating wireframes, and finally designing the UI. I have been constantly working on help them improving the user experience of web, android, internal & partners tools.

Before joining PaySense I was working as a part-time freelancer designer for almost 4 years and worked with a number of startups, companies and individuals. Some of the recent clients include Eatzer, Alikemom, HotelBids, Picpulse and SkillFab. I have successfully delivered all sorts of projects during this time like UI/UX, Illustrations, Graphics, Branding as well as Front-end development.
